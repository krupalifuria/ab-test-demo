Conservation Methods & Resources

Tips for Ethical Living

  • Reduce your carbon footprint (prioritize public transport/bike).
  • Avoid purchasing products made from endangered species.
  • Support sustainable agriculture and fair-trade products.
  • Minimize plastic use and recycle diligently.
  • Be a responsible tourist when visiting natural areas.

Conservation Tools & Resources

  • **IUCN Red List:** Global database for species status.
  • **Project Tiger:** India's flagship program for big cat protection.
  • **WTI & WWF:** Access their resource libraries for detailed reports.
  • **Citizen Science Apps:** Report sightings to help researchers.

Advanced Conservation Methods

  • **Habitat Corridor Development:** Connecting fragmented forest patches.
  • **Bio-Fencing:** Using natural barriers to prevent human-wildlife conflict.
  • **Anti-Poaching Tech:** Utilizing thermal drones and satellite monitoring.
  • **Genetic Banking:** Preservation of genetic material for future reintroduction.

Act Today: Report Illegal Wildlife Activities

If you see suspicious activity related to poaching or illegal trade, report it immediately to local authorities or recognized wildlife protection agencies. Your vigilance can save lives.
